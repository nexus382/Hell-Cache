## Playstyle Archetypes

### Glass Cannon
**Focus**: Maximum burst damage
**Spells**: Magma Strike, Fireball, Death Ray, Mana Flare
**Stats**: High damage/power, negative defense

**Loadout**: Fireball + Mana Flare
- Cast Mana Flare (free next spell)
- Cast Fireball for 0 mana, huge burst

### Kiting Mage
**Focus**: Speed and range, hit-and-run
**Spells**: Lightning Bolt, Ice Bolt, Arcane Bolt, Haste, Teleport
**Stats**: High speed/agility, moderate damage

**Loadout**: Lightning Bolt + Haste
- Cast Haste for +50% speed
- Lightning Bolt pierces, kite enemies

### Control Mage
**Focus**: Crowd control and debuffs
**Spells**: Frost Nova, Frost Beam, Void Zone, Venom Dart
**Stats**: High power, balanced damage/speed

**Loadout**: Frost Nova + Void Zone
- Cast Void Zone to group enemies
- Frost Nova to freeze entire group

### Battle Mage
**Focus**: Survivability and sustained damage
**Spells**: Life Drain, Arcane Shield, Phase Shift, Chain Lightning
**Stats**: High defense/power, moderate speed

**Loadout**: Life Drain + Arcane Shield
- Cast Arcane Shield for 30 damage absorb
- Channel Life Drain to heal back

### AOE Specialist
**Focus**: Maximum area damage
**Spells**: Meteor Shower, Chain Lightning, Frost Nova, Void Zone
**Stats**: High power/range, negative speed

**Loadout**: Meteor Shower + Chain Lightning
- Cast Meteor Shower at range
- Chain Lightning cleans up survivors
